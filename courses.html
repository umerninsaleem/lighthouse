Courses

Literature (English)

Literature is more than language — it’s the art of survival through stories. From poetry to essays, we explore the power of expression and reflection.

Math (Basic Concepts)

Math is the quiet language behind how everything works. With basics and real-world problem solving, we make math simple, beautiful, and alive.

Biology

Biology teaches us how life breathes, heals, and evolves. From cells to ecosystems, we explore how life is beautifully interconnected.

Physics

Physics is curiosity in motion — a way to ask, “Why does this happen?” and explore the universe’s most fascinating rules.

Chemistry

Chemistry helps us understand change — in matter and mindset. We make it visual, relevant, and empowering.

Astrophysics

Astrophysics invites us to think bigger than ourselves, to look up and wonder. We explore black holes, galaxies, and the secrets of the universe.
